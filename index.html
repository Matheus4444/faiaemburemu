Fire Emblem Three Houses: The Black Eagles

The Black Eagles (黒鷲の学級アドラークラッセ Adorākurasse) are one of the three student houses at the Garreg Mach Monastery Officers Academy in Fire Emblem: Three Houses.

The Black Eagles house at the Officers Academy is comprised of students who hail from the Adrestian Empire. They favor the use of axes and black magic.

The twin-headed eagle of the Adrestian Empire's coat of arms and the black armor worn by Imperial soldiers inspired the name and banner of the Black Eagles.

The Black Eagles don't have as many commoners among their ranks, with Dorothea being an exception (Technically speaking, Petra is also a commoner, but according to official lore, she's a princess in her homeland).

Black Eagles Post-timeskip

After the events that led to the timeskip, the former Black Eagles class reorganized themselves as the Black Eagle Strike Force.

The Black Eagle Strike Force (BESF) is composed by the elite of the elite of the Adrestian Empire military, including the Empress herself, and they're all guided by their Strategist and professor, Byleth.
